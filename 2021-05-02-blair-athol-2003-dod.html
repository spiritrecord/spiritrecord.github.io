Blair Athol 2003 Douglas of Drumlanrig 12 years 46% (refill hogshead)

Presumably an exbourbon refill hoggy. One of Diageoâ€™s blend focused distilleries.

Colour Light straw.

Nose Fresh fruits and bananas, perfume and menthol. Boiled sweets and sweetened cream. Heady, alcoholic, surprising for a 46 per cent.

Palate Caramel. A slight astringency. Malt, bright acidic oranges. Oily zest. Pretty light, a little grassy and slightly floral.

Finish Nutty, coconut. A little oak and some bitter herbs. Moderate.

Comments Pretty light on. Overall, too simple and dull. Perfumed nose and the zesty notes carry most of the interest. 75/100.
