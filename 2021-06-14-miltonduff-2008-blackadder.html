Miltonduff 2008 Blackadder 10 years 60% (hogshead)

Typo on the bottle, says 2006. Hogshead 700950.

Colour Gold.

Nose Pineapple and coconut, peaches and cream. Caramel and toffee apples. Malty and creamy. Dried pawpaw. Quite tropical. Icing sugar and almond crescents. Hints of dried fruits. Buttery, passionfruit.

Palate Pineapple juice and caramel. Malty. Rockmelon and pawpaw. Really quite sweet, apple and orange juice. Malty. With water, herbaceous, a little floral. Gardeny notes.

Finish Caramel and cream. Passionfruit sorbet, banana sundae. With water, some peppermint and lemongrass.

Comments Quite a tropical dram. Buttery. Beginning to think this is a classic Miltonduff profile. 85/100.
